Where Do I Begin?

Here is a very effective and simple routine. There is a lot of buzz around the 10-step Korean routine and there is certainly a reason for it. However, if you are starting into skincare, chances are, your skin is not prepared for that "marathon" of active ingredients.
My advice is to follow a routine that adapts to your skin needs and life-style (here you should ask yourself how much time are you prepared to give away to the gods of skincare).
